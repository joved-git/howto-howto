Here are the 5 steps during an intrusion test:

1. Discovery: collect information on the target. As known as Reconnaissance.
2. Enumeration: network cartography (service and open ports, ...).
3. Exploit: enter the system, profit the vulnerability.
4. Maintain future access: hide our traces (footsteps), prepare easier access, privilege elevation.
5. Report and consequence: make a report and propose solution.
