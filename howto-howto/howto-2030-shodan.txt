Shodan is a big site that collects and presents information about conencted objets.

[ product:minecraft ] or Explore/Video Games gives information about popular video game servers.
        example: Minecraft

[ ip webcam ] find connected webcam. !!! Shodan is able to detect honeypot and shows them [honeypot].

[ country:fr ] filter by country.

!!! https://github.com/JavierOlmedo/shodan-filters gives all the possible filters.
